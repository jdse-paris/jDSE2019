Directions

By public transport

From Paris, take the line RER B to Saint-Remy-lès-Chevreuse. You have three options:

  • Stop at Massy-Palaiseau and take the bus number 91.06 (A, B or C) or 91.10. Get off at the bus stop "Université Paris-Saclay".
  • Stop at Orsay Ville and take the bus number 7. Get off at the bus stop "Université Paris-Saclay".
  • Stop at Le Guichet and take the bus number 9. Get off at the bus stop "Université Paris-Saclay".

By car

  • Departure from Paris, périphérique - Porte de Saint Cloud:
    • Take the N118 towards Nantes-Bordeaux.
    • After around 20 km, take the exit 9 "Centre Universitaire".
    • Follow the road signs at the roundabout.
  • Departure from Paris via A6-A10:
    • Drive towards Nantes-Bordeaux, then drive towards "Cité Scientifique" (left lane) and then Saclay.
    • Drive on D36, then merge onto D128 and drive towards "Centre Universitaire".
    • Drive through 2 roundabouts. At the third roundabout, take the first exit on the right (Gif-sur-Yvette). After 500 m, take the first exit on the left.
  • From the south via A10:
    • Take the N118 towards Paris-Porte de Saint-Cloud.
    • Take the exit 9 "Centre Universitaire".
    • At the roundabout, take the first exit on the right (Gif-sur-Yvette). Then after 500 m, take the first exit on the left.
  • From the south via A6:
    • Take the N104 ("Francilienne") towards Versailles until the motorway interchange with A10.
    • Take the exit 9 "Centre Universitaire".
    • At the roundabout, take the first exit on the right (Gif-sur-Yvette). Then after 500 m, take the first exit on the left.
